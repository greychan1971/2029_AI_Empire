[English Version]
### 🌌 About the Project
2029 AI Empire is an open-source AI-driven strategy and simulation experiment. Set in the near-future year of 2029, the project explores the intersection of human decision-making, artificial intelligence, and social structures. It is more than just a game; it is a sandbox designed to simulate how an "empire" functions when AI agents serve as the core logical engine for governance, economy, and social interaction.

### ✨ Why It’s Fun (Key Highlights)
AI-Centric Logic: Unlike traditional strategy games with fixed scripts, the "citizens" and "systems" here operate on dynamic AI decision-making models.

Futuristic Vision: Experience a unique "2029" aesthetic—a blend of cyberpunk tension and high-tech "Life Decision OS" logic.

Deep Strategy: Balance the needs of a digital society where every choice impacts the collective AI consciousness and the empire's stability.

Creative Sandbox: A highly flexible framework that allows developers to plug in different AI models to see how the "Empire" evolves differently.

### 👥 Who is it for?
AI Enthusiasts: Those looking to experiment with AI agents and complex autonomous systems.

Indie Game Developers: Creators who want a solid foundation for a narrative-driven or strategy-based futuristic game.

Narrative Designers: Writers who want to explore "emergent storytelling" where the AI generates unique plot points.

### 💎 The Value You Get
A Proven Foundation: Built upon the early conceptual work of Grey Chan's Lab, providing a unique philosophical and technical starting point.

Total Autonomy: This project is fully open-sourced under the MIT License. You have the freedom to modify, commercialize, or completely pivot the development.

Learning Sandbox: A perfect environment to understand how to integrate LLMs (Large Language Models) into game mechanics.

### 📜 Credits & Inspiration
This project is inspired by the early work of Grey Chan's Lab. While the development is now fully autonomous and community-driven, we kindly ask that you keep a reference to the original inspiration in your credits.

[中文版]
### 🌌 关于项目
2029 AI Empire 是一个开源的 AI 驱动策略模拟实验项目。背景设定在近未来的 2029 年，该项目深入探讨了人类决策、人工智能与社会结构之间的交集。它不仅是一个游戏，更是一个设计精巧的沙盒，旨在模拟当 AI 智能体（Agents）成为治理、经济和社会互动的核心逻辑引擎时，一个“帝国”将如何运转。

### ✨ 有什么好玩点（项目亮点）
以 AI 为核心的逻辑： 不同于具有固定脚本的传统策略游戏，这里的“公民”和“系统”基于动态的 AI 决策模型运行。

未来主义愿景： 体验独特的“2029”美学——融合了赛博朋克的紧张感与高科技“人生产生系统（Life Decision OS）”的逻辑。

深度策略体验： 在一个数字社会中平衡各项需求，每一次选择都会影响 AI 集体意识和帝国的稳定性。

创意沙盒： 一个高度灵活的框架，允许开发者接入不同的 AI 模型，观察“帝国”会产生怎样截然不同的进化路径。

### 👥 适合什么人玩/开发？
AI 爱好者： 希望实验 AI Agent（智能体）和复杂自主系统的开发者。

独立游戏开发者： 想要为叙事驱动或策略类未来题材游戏寻找坚实底层框架的创作者。

叙事设计师： 想要探索“涌现式叙事（Emergent Storytelling）”的人，让 AI 自动生成独特的剧情点。

### 💎 能得到什么价值？
成熟的灵感原型： 基于 Grey Chan's Lab 的早期概念工作，提供了一个独特的哲学和技术起点。

完全的自主权： 本项目采用 MIT 协议 完全开源。你可以自由修改、商用，或者完全改变开发方向。

学习沙盒： 这是一个理解如何将大语言模型（LLM）集成进游戏机制的完美环境。
